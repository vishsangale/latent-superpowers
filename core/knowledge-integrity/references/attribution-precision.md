# Attribution Precision

Do not muddy the waters of academic attribution. 
When explaining where an idea originated, do not lazily credit the most popular paper if a prior technical paper laid the foundation.

## Guidelines
- Use "Introduced by" only for the proven original authors of the mathematical or architectural concept.
- Use "Popularized by" for papers that scaled the concept or demonstrated its efficacy on a famous benchmark.
- Example: "Residual connections were popularized in ImageNet models by He et al. (ResNet), but Highway Networks (Srivastava et al.) introduced earlier successful training of very deep networks with bypass paths."
